About The Modern Frame

Our History

Founded in 2010, The Modern Frame began as a small studio dedicated to promoting local talent. Over the past decade, we have grown into a respected institution within the art community, known for our bold curation and commitment to artistic innovation. Our mission is to bridge the gap between artists and collectors, fostering a vibrant cultural dialogue.

"Art washes away from the soul the dust of everyday life." - Pablo Picasso
